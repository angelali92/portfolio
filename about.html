Who is Angela?

Artist at heart, programmer in application.

Angela enjoys contributing to the creative arts. For the past two years, she's been doing this by working as a Pipeline Technical Director for House of Moves, a motion capture and animation studio based in El Segundo.

Originally from San Francisco, she traveled down to Southern California for college and graduated from UC Irvine with a B.S. in Computer Science and Visual Computing specialization.

In her spare time, she likes playing games, watching movies, reading, dancing, and practicing yoga.
